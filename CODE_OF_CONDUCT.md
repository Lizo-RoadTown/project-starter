# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is maintained by **Liz Osborn** as a public, community-contributable resource. Everyone participating — issue authors, PR contributors, commenters, and maintainers — agrees to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on the technical substance of contributions
- Disagree without being personal
- Assume good intent until clearly proven otherwise

## What's not acceptable

- Personal attacks, slurs, or harassment of any kind
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of issues, PRs, or discussions
- Promoting hate speech or violence

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, PRs, commit messages, discussions, and any official communication channel — and when individuals are representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

If you see behavior that violates this code:

1. **Direct issues:** if you can address the behavior directly and feel safe doing so, that's the first step
2. **Escalate to the maintainer:** report to Liz Osborn via the email listed in the repo, or by opening a private security advisory if confidentiality is needed

The maintainer will review and take action proportionate to the violation. Possible actions: a comment correction, a temporary or permanent ban from the project, deletion of comments.
